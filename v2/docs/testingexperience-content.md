# Testing Experience Magazine - Website Content Analysis

## Website Overview
**URL**: https://www.testingexperience.media/
**Platform**: Wix-based digital publication

Testing Experience is a digital magazine dedicated to software testing, quality assurance, and quality engineering. It serves as a knowledge-sharing platform for testing professionals, providing insights, articles, and best practices from industry experts.
