Executive Summary

Live Shopping & Social Commerce introduces real-time video streaming with integrated e-commerce, requiring exceptional focus on Security (payment processing, UGC moderation), Reliability (stream stability, notification delivery), and Performance (video latency, concurrent viewers). The combination of live events, third-party social integrations (Instagram, TikTok), and in-video purchasing creates a complex quality landscape where failures are highly visible to engaged Gen-Z audiences.

  • Reliability: Payment gateway resilience, database transaction integrity
  • Usability: WCAG 1.2.4 caption compliance (E1 dependency)

⚡ Quick Wins

  • Add CDN failover with 3-second recovery target
  • Configure rate limiting with proper Retry-After headers
  • Enable live indicator visibility testing at 1366x768

Security
"How well protected is the product against unauthorized use or access?"

💡 Why This Matters

Live Shopping introduces significant security surface area: in-video payment processing handles financial transactions during emotionally-charged live events; UGC integration from Instagram/TikTok requires content moderation to prevent brand damage; and user notification preferences contain PII requiring GDPR protection. The combination of real-time transactions and social media integration creates attack vectors for XSS through UGC captions, CSRF on "Remind Me" buttons, and SQL injection in event search.

Business Impact: Payment security breaches during live celebrity events would cause immediate reputational damage. E-commerce fraud costs merchants approximately 3.75% of revenue (Source: LexisNexis True Cost of Fraud Study, 2023). UGC moderation failures could expose the platform to legal liability and brand partnerships at risk with LeGer/GMK celebrity collections.
