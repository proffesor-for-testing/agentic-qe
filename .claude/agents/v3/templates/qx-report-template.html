How can this report help you?

▼
"Quality is value to some person who matters." - Jerry Weinberg / James Bach

This QX (Quality Experience) report uses AI-powered analysis to evaluate your platform through both Quality Advocacy (QA) and User Experience (UX) perspectives simultaneously - bridging the traditional divide between "does it work?" and "is it delightful?"

Unlike traditional testing reports that focus only on defects, or UX audits that focus only on user satisfaction, QX analysis identifies oracle problems where quality criteria conflict between different stakeholders.

Use this report to facilitate discussions about what quality means for each stakeholder, identify hidden conflicts in your requirements, and prioritize improvements that deliver value to everyone who matters.

When to perform a QX session?

▼

Platform Redesign or Migration: When redesigning core user journeys or consolidating systems, QX analysis helps identify where stakeholder needs conflict.

Expansion to New Markets: When adding localization or new user personas, QX reveals hidden oracle problems around "correct" behavior that differs by context.

Serving Vulnerable Populations: When your users include those with disabilities, mental health conditions, or other vulnerabilities, QX ensures accessibility isn't sacrificed for aesthetics.

Regulatory Compliance: For healthcare, finance, or regulated industries - QX ensures compliance doesn't come at the expense of user experience.
